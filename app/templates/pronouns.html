This tool takes advantage of a very simple approach to expose stereotypes: If you wouldn't write or say the sentence in the same way for the other binary gender, it is likely to include stereotypes. The exceptions are of course inherently gendered words. This tool shows bias on the level of sentences and paragraphs, not on word-level.
Also, if you don't want to choose pronouns that reflect a non-binary gender, one option is to use "they" as a neutral pronoun. Your input is also returned in this way to represent this alternative.

  • Use pronouns
  • For "they": Only the pronouns of the first/main subject get changed
