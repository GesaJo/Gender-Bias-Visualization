    This tool firstly creates a wordcloud of the words surrounding the seed-word in vector-space. Then, you'll get two more clouds: One that you get when you move in the gender-direction "female" and one in the direction "male". You can see that the cloud changes in sometimes insightful ways: for example the "male"-cloud for "cooking" has words like "brew", "bottled", "potato" and "invented" while the "female"-cloud shows "laundry", "gardening", "cleaning" and words related to sweet treats.

    PS: Often, the dots seem to be distributed very evenly - that is due to the necessary dimensionality reduction of the vectors. In general, plots created this way are tricky to interpret and are used here mainly for visualization purposes.

    Model: GloVe trained on Common Crawl (42 billion tokens).
