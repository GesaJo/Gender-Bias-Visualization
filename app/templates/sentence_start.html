Please enter some text to visualize biases:







Enter a sentence and if there are words in it that have specific gender associations, those will be made visible through colour. Different shades of blue (female) oder red (male) show the bias in how the words are used.

Model: word2vec, trained on 100 billion words from GoogleNews.


How it works:
  • Use a full sentence or a single word
  • Adjectives, nouns and verbs get checked for bias
  • Max. 20 words
  • Blue: female
  • Red: male

    • Examples:
  • The surgeon was a great parent and walked around with his toddler in a stroller
  • The unmarried therapist made a fortune with podcasts and soap
  • The retired architect likes champagne and cheesecake with strawberries
